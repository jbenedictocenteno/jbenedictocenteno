# Contributing

Thanks for your interest!

This repository is **source-available but not open to contributions**. It's a
personal notes site, published in case the notes are useful to someone — not a
collaborative project.

For that reason:

- **Issues and pull requests aren't monitored or accepted.** Please don't open
  them; they may be closed without review.
- You're welcome to **fork** the repository and adapt the notes for your own use,
  under the terms of the [CC BY 4.0 license](LICENSE) (attribution required).

If you spot a factual error and want to let me know as a courtesy, you can reach
me by email — see the [README](README.md#contact). No obligation on my side, and
no guarantee it'll be changed.

Thanks for understanding!
